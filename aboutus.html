About Us

Our team was established in a Harvey Mudd College Stem and Social Impact Class to address the problem of recycling habits on college campuses. This website was started as a way to increase awareness about recyling and plastics recycling, specifically on college campuses.

Purpose

We feel that there is a large amount of confusion around recycling, and as college students, we are some of the most frequent users of plastic. Everyday students can be seen improperly recycling in the dining halls and at the dorms, and this leads to contamination, and an overall less efficient recycling process.
